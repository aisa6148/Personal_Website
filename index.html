I am a 1st year Ph.D. student working with Dr. Robin Burke. My research focuses on news recommendation systems, and I am looking into exploring the bigger question of how journalistic voices can be manifested in news recommender systems. I aspire to develop a recommendation framework that not only optimizes personalization and relevance but also upholds the democratic principles and ethical standards essential for the media ecosystem.
